A student was [found with 2 guns at Enloe High School][3], where my sisters went to school. This is following a [fatal shooting][1] at a high school in Winston-Salem, not too far from here, only a day earlier. That shooting was just [after one at a high school in Wilmington][2], where there were no deaths but injuries. Two high school shootings and a potential third averted in this state, just *this week*. 

My son just stared going to high school after doing the virtual thing his freshman year. How could I not worry about him?

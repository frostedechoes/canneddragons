# Black Marble "Somewhere"

This was a pleasant surprise in my Apple New Music Mix last week. I haven't heard any Black Marble in a while (I enjoyed some tracks on 2016's *It's Immaterial*). "Somewhere" starts off with some pretty standard synth swells, and proceeds along like a synthstramental for about a minute and half before kicking into an upbeat new wave/post-punk bopper.[^1] The video is fun, with a kid getting to hang and dance with the cool adults.

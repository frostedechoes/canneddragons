**MUNYA** is set to release her third EP, *Voyage to Mars*, in November. Sounding much like fellow Quebecois, Men I Trust, she brings deep grooves and a breezy delivery. Sometimes, such as on "Boca Chica" throwing in a little tropicalia-tinged space-age bachelor pad ambience. 

The first song I heard was the cover of the Smashing Pumpkins "Tonight, Tonight."

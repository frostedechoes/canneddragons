Obsidian has a [new default theme](https://forum.obsidian.md/t/obsidian-release-v0-16-0-insider-build/42536) called "Dragonglass." The theme has only been released in the insider builds, so I haven't had a chance to play with it. It looks appealing, though. I like the idea of tabs, which have been adopted in many other apps (at least on MacOS). I also appreciate having a theme that looks OS native and doesn't require community plugins (which bring security concerns with them) to achieve that look.

# Romeo and Juliet

<p>I remember hearing Widowspeak years ago, when their album <em>Almanac</em> came out and not getting too attached to it. When Apple Music recommended a song from their new EP, I gave the band another try in the flow of a new music playlist. I quickly reversed the direction of the playlist and returned to revisit the track. I listened to “Romeo and Juliet” about a dozen times today. </p>


<p>If it weren’t for the lyrics, it would be hard to believe this is a Dire Straits cover and not a straight take on a Mazzy Star song. In the context of the music, the lyrics make the beginning of the track sound like something from one of those 90’s “alternative” movies like <em>Reality Bites</em>. It certainly doesn’t bring to mind the Sultans of Swing. The cliches are easily overlooked when the atmosphere is this beautiful. </p>

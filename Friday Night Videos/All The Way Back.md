# All The Way Back

I had heard Divine Sweater before, but I was still pleasantly surprised by their new track when it popped up. A powerful pop tune with a tight groove, "All The Way Back" starts to charm the listener about 25 seconds in, after a dreamy guitar intro. The song has a subtle bombast. As paradoxical as that sounds, somehow Divine Sweater manages to pull it off with sweetness. The vocal harmonies during the chorus glide over smoothly as a sort of respite from the force of the verses. 

All in all, a good way to start the weekend.

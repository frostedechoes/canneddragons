# Yumi Zouma "In Camera"

"In Camera" is just one of the many Balearic, breezy Yumi Zouma songs worthy of the single/video treatment. Boy, does it get it. This story of a square (by retro standards) boy who goes to a girl's house for a date and gets treated to a dance routine is well choreographed. The dance itself involves a little *Flashdance*, a little Steve Martin King Tut and just a splash of Chris Farley as a Chippendale's dancer. When the dance evolves to synchronized swimming, it's on. I kept waiting for the couple to kiss at the end...

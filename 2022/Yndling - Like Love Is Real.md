# Yndling "Like Love Is Real"

This track by Norway's Yndling has been a standout on my *Now Sounds* playlist. 
Perfect shoegaze with otherworldly tones and ethereal vocals. A bass line that seems to wrap the song in a bow. It's rare that I like a song more every time I listen to it, but this is such a track.
